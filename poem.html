VOICED SILENCE



In a timeless realm , profoundly outspaced ,

Neither knew the thrill of love's glaze ,

Nor the warmth that sets the soul ablaze !!

When days were filled with profound delight ,

No shadows of passion , no sleepless nights !!

Was Miserably unaware of love's tender art ,

Just kept roaming the world

with an ungraded heart ...



When I Witnessed a fairy tale , unfold in real life ,

It all felt like dreaming all day long , for was clueless ,

" what's going on " ...

Felt lost in those eyes which seemed to have held galaxies within ,

The aura felt like a raiment waved with golden string.

But somewhere it felt like forgotten paths ,

As Déjá vu kept rending my fragile heart apart !!



With each passing day , I found myself lost ,

For I deem u as , " The Maestro of an Unseen art " ,

Weaving emotions in complex unspoken sonnets ,

Where every brushstroke of your presence paints a narrative ,

In the gallery of my soul ....

Your artistry there , still beholds !!

For I was still , " A parasite " ,

Carring your embrace , yet feeding on you ,

Living for the essence of your existence ,

Still Calling it a Part behold of you !!
