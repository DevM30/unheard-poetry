SOLITUDE'S VEIL



Wherefore dost mountains sighed down the whispers of thy self ,

When thine prayers bled down the cascading waters ,

Thine essence never thy found frozen ,

for it's empathy provided radiance down the infinite ally ...

Of twilight's veil , where thou shadows convene ,

And stars dost murmur the tales of ages past , forgotten ,

The moon's soft serene doth guided the soul through shadow's dread .



’neath the canopy of dreams where murmur doth weaved

, A trapsery of hope ..

Broken , once thine woke up , found thyslef all alone.

In the fields where lilies bloom , untouched by hand

, Their vibrant scent of purity doth fills the air .

For in stillness , where all echoes fade ,

The spirit findeth voice , an ancient aid ,

doth making every heart beat .

The nightingale doth sing a soft refrain ,

A melody for the weary heart , ’neath shadow's embrace ,

free from mortal pain ,

Love's eternal flame doth never defame ...
