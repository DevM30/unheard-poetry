LOVE'S EMBRACE



I blame her ghost , when she doesn't visit me .

Perhaps the spectrum is more aware of her reproach

Oh my kind , tell her about me ,

That Longing made me lose my ease ...

She taught me the way I can cut the roots of passion from depth ,

She taught me How tears die in a mourning breath ,



Cuz no matter how much the sun and the moon might dwell in the sea ,

still the sea ensures every second to touch the shore .

Cuz the lover dies , the love doesn't and the soul leaves , the embrace doesn't ..

Yet no matter how romantic the season of monsoon is

Its beauty masks the chaos that often accompanies bliss ...

Cuz true devotion is a lighthouse in the storm ,

While betrayal is like disastrous phantom wind .



They say : " love is not up to the mark without a radiant allure " .

But I believe : " The heart is easily deceived, for the deceiver dwells within " .

They say : " A fair countenance doth deceive tender heart " .

But I believe : " True love decends beyond outward art "...
